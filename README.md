# Zucchini

Zucchini is now a small pnpm workspace monorepo with:

- `apps/desktop`: the Electron habit tracker
- `apps/web`: the marketing/download site

## Tooling

- Node 22.x
- pnpm 10.27.0

The desktop app is a local-first habit tracker for people who want momentum,
not micromanagement.

It gives you a focused daily view, gentle reminders, streak protection, and
clear history so you can keep showing up without handing your routines over to
another cloud service.

## Why Zucchini

- Local-first by default, with your data stored on your machine.
- Fast daily check-ins with habits built around real schedules.
- Streaks and freezes that reward consistency without punishing one bad day.
- Visual history and weekly reviews that make progress easy to see.
- Desktop reminders and tray behavior that keep the app useful throughout the
  day.

## What You Get

- A Today view for quickly checking off daily and longer-cycle habits.
- Weekly review cards and charts for spotting trends before motivation drops.
- Flexible habit management with categories, frequencies, and reordering.
- Reminder settings for staying on track without keeping the app front and
  center.
- A desktop app built with Electron, React, TypeScript, and SQLite.

## Built For Real Life

Zucchini is designed for personal systems that need to survive busy days,
missed days, and imperfect routines. The app keeps the interface lightweight,
stores progress locally, and focuses on clear feedback instead of noisy
gamification.
